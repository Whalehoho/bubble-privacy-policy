Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2025

Bubble App Launcher respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All user settings are stored locally on the device.

1. App Functionality

The app displays a floating bubble on your screen. When tapped, it shows a 3x3 pattern lock screen. Drawing a valid pattern launches the user’s assigned app. All configurations are stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences.

2. Permissions

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW – Required to display the floating bubble over other apps.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES or usage access – Used to list apps so you can assign them to patterns.

3. Data Collection

This app does not collect or transmit any data externally. No analytics or third-party SDKs are used. Pattern data and app shortcut preferences remain on your device only.

4. Third-Party Access

This app does not share data with any third parties.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page and in future updates.
